> Life happens at 1x. Every conversation you’ve ever had. Every walk, every meal, every meaningful experience. None of it comes with a speed dial. We’re biological creatures wired for real-time processing.

> Your brain needs empty space too. This is the part we’ve collectively forgotten. Boredom is a feature, not a bug. 

The secret is slowing down.
